OUR MOTTO


The COVID-19 pandemic has set the entire world into an unprecendented situation. There is negativity all around us. Many people are getting affected by this highly contagious virus and many are losing their near and dear ones and are going through severe mental stress. We are Friend-In-Need (FIN), an online COVID-19 support group, which organises weekly group discussions, counselling services, entertainment events and loads of other activities. We also have study groups, gaming groups and other groups which people can join and interact with others sharing the same interest. Our aim is to provide people with few moments of joy and happiness in these dark times so that they get rejuvenated and motivated to fight this pandemic with full vigour.
